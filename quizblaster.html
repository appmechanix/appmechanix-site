Introducing...

QuizBlaster is a game for lovers of trivia, arcane facts and just plain esoteric randomness. Our personal experience tells us people just love to show off their extensive general knowledge...and QuizBlaster allows that to happen on a global scale!

Everywhere

QuizBlaster is really simple – just answer the questions as quickly as you can. You can play on iPhone, iPad, Windows Phone or Windows PC.

Contribute

A general knowledge quiz is only as good as its questions. We have a stunning question database but we’re always adding fresh content – and you can contribute questions and earn points at the same time.
